Welcome To MECL

Provides Geological Services

Mineral Exploration and Consultancy Limited (MECL) (formerly Mineral Exploration Corporation Limited) was set up as an organization under the administrative control of the Ministry of Mines, Government of India for systematic exploration of minerals to reduce the gap between discovery of a prospecting site and its eventual exploitation. Incorporated as an autonomous public company in October, 1972. The mission of MECL is – “To provide high quality, cost effective and time bound geo-scientific services for exploration and exploitation of minerals.”
